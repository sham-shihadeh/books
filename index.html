The name of the website "bookra" comes from the English word "book" and the Arabic name "Iqraa اقرأ" which means to read. Also, the word "bookra بكرا " means tomorrow in Arabic.

The idea of creating a book website is that I wanted to learn how to implement my love for reading in Computer Science. in the next section of the page you will find some of my favourite qoutes from books I had read.
